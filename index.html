laser trip wire alarm security system

A laser trip wire alarm security system typically consists of a laser emitter and a receiver placed opposite each other to create an invisible beam. When the beam is interrupted by an object passing through it, it triggers an alarm.

smoke detector

A smoke detector is a device that senses smoke and alerts occupants of a building, typically to the presence of fire. It works by either detecting smoke particles in the air or changes in the level of light caused by smoke. Smoke detectors are crucial for fire safety
